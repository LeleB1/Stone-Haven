What We Do:

We provide hands-on, professional care for Jerusalem apartments owned by people living abroad.

  • Ongoing apartment management & inspections.
  • Maintenance & coordination with trusted professionals.
  • Cleaning & apartment preparation before arrival.
  • Grocery shopping & home setup.
  • Family support services, including babysitter coordination.

You get one reliable local contact who takes care of everything.

How It Works:

  1. Tell us about your home:

    You share your apartment details, needs, and schedule.

  2. Choose a care plan:

    Monthly management or one-time services — flexible and clear.

  3. We take care of the rest:

    We manage your home as if it were our own, with photo updates and full transparency.

Property Management & Inspections
  • Regular apartment checks (monthly or quarterly)
  • Ventilation, water flow, leak detection
  • Mail handling
  • Bill coordination (Arnona, Vaad Bayit, utilities)
  • Detailed reports with photos and videos
Maintenance & Repairs

We work only with trusted, vetted professionals, including:

  • Plumbers
  • Electricians
  • Air-conditioning technicians
  • Painters & handymen

We coordinate, supervise, and report — so you don’t have to manage anything remotely
Cleaning & Organization
  • Deep cleaning before arrival
  • Periodic cleaning while apartment is vacant
  • Post-renovation cleaning
  • Linen change, laundry, and closet organization
Pre-Arrival Apartment Setup

Arrive to a home that’s ready for you

  • Full cleaning before arrival
  • Fresh linens & towels
  • Grocery shopping based on your list
  • Heating, hot water, and air conditioning turned on
  • Key coordination and access setup
Family & Personal Support

For families visiting Jerusalem:

  • Babysitter coordination
  • Trusted, vetted caregivers
  • Flexible scheduling (evenings / short stays)
