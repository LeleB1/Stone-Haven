About Us

We are a Jerusalem-based home and property management company dedicated to supporting property owners who live abroad. We understand that owning a home in another country comes with unique challenges - from day-to-day maintenance to preparing a home for your arrival and ensuring everything runs smoothly while you are away. Our services cover both the essentials and the extras. From regular property oversight, maintenance coordination, and vendor management, to personalized services such as grocery shopping before arrival, babysitting coordination, and catering arrangements, we take care of the details so you can enjoy peace of mind. Whether your home is used seasonally, rented occasionally, or simply waiting for your next visit, we treat every property with care, responsibility, and attention - as if it were our own.

Why Us?

We are proud members of the American Israeli community in Jerusalem, and we know firsthand the realities of owning property here while living abroad. We understand the cultural, logistical, and communication gaps that many overseas homeowners face - and we know how to bridge them. As students and professionals studying and working in architecture and interior design, we bring insider knowledge to every home we manage. This allows us to look beyond basic maintenance and understand how homes function, age, and require care - from materials and finishes to long-term upkeep and design integrity. Our background gives us the ability to:
  • Communicate clearly with contractors and service providers.
  • Identify issues before they become problems.
  • Ensure your home is maintained with both function and aesthetics in mind.
